This is data you knowingly and actively provide when you use or participate in any of our services and promotions.

2.2. Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit our Site, our servers may automatically log standard data provided by your web browser. This data is considered "non-identifying information," as it does not personally identify you on its own.

  • Log and Device Data: This may include your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, your browser type and version, the pages you visit on our Site, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on each page, and other technical details about your device and operating system.

This service collects information such as how often users visit, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to ours. We use this data to improve our Service, but it is collected in an aggregated and anonymized form.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies, like the Facebook Pixel, to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. This is explained in detail in Section 5. These technologies help us with performance analytics, maintaining your session, and for marketing and advertising purposes.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide and improve our Service. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device that helps us remember information about your visit.

5.1. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as user login, shopping cart management, and payment processing.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies, set by services like Google Analytics, allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors and see how they move around our site. This helps us improve the way our website works.
  • Marketing and Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to make advertising more relevant to you. The Facebook Pixel is an example. It helps us track conversions from Facebook ads, optimize ads, and build targeted audiences for future ads (retargeting).

5.2. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. However, you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of our Services. You can typically find these settings in the "options" or "preferences" menu of your browser. For more information, please consult the help documentation for your browser.

The security of your data is a top priority. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These include:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to and from our Site is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.

  • Secure Partners: We rely on trusted third-party providers like FastSpring who are industry leaders in security and compliance (e.g., PCI DSS compliant).
